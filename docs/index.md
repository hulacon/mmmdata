# Multi-Modal Memory Dataset (MMMData)

A dense fMRI study investigating how the brain remembers sights and sounds
across traditional experimental and naturalistic settings, conducted at the
University of Oregon.

Study design, experimental tasks, stimuli, MRI acquisition parameters, BIDS
